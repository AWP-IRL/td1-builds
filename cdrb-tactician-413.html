This build is specifically for Tactician skill users running PvE activities solo. The stat distribution for this build is roughly 4/4/8 as going to at least 3832 Firearms and Stamina allows you to meet requirements for various talents including Destructive and Ferocious. In a perfect world, you would use five gear mods with Damage to Elites to be able to reach 3832 Firearms. Since the only good ones with 4% Damage to Elites recently sold came with 193 Firearms, you have to switch one of the gear mods to a high end one in order to hit the 3832 Firearms requirement. That high end mod will have 3% skill haste on it as the main attribute.

Primary Weapon:

Your primary weapon will be Lightweight M4/LVOA-C, whichever one you prefer. The top two talents must be Destructive and Ferocious due to the fact that they also apply to your skills while you are holding the weapons with the talents. The free talent I prefer to have on my primary is Predatory due to the fact that it also activates upon skill kills which can be a huge boost as this build has no healing skills equipped relying solely on medkits and Nimble.
