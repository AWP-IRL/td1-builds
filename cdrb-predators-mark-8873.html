The first weapon i use for utility. In general i can bleed them fast. To make a highjer possibility to trigger shrapnel and better spread of bleed u need to swap targets if u land 10 bullets on each target there is a 30 % chance to proc shrapnel, so more targets bleeding gives a higher chance of procing shrapnel. its a good support build and its tanking wich allows u to play relatively aggressively but u dont have stacks like with striker so u rely on predatory for some sustain.
