Build Guide

This build works well for both solo and group PvE play. It is aimed at the sticky bomb being able to nuke down targets quickly. You can run it with any weapons you like that have determined/ferocious/destructive, and the weapon mods are personal preference. If you don't have DtE mods yet, run skill power or skill haste instead. Don't be afraid to use your signature skill, determined gets it back pretty quick. Roll the gloves' weapon damage to what ever you use as your primary.

This build can be modified to run any other skills. Either with another damage skill, or as a support build. For a support build, switch the Enemy Armour Damage rolls to skill haste. If your team does not have a healer, just switch any skill to Life Support station and change Chain Reaction to Tech support.

Skill options:

Replace sticky with turret: Four Turret Damage mods, change Chain Reaction to Tech Support.

Replace sticky with seeker mines: Four Seeker Mine Damage mods

Change BFB sticky to Flashbang or Disruptor, change Chain Reaction to Precision.

Change Overdose to Defibrillator or Booster Shot: Four First Aid Ally Heal mods, change Chain Reaction to Triage if not running BFB.

Replace sticky with Recon Pack Pulse: Four Pulse Critical Hit Damage mods, change Chain Reaction to Precision.
